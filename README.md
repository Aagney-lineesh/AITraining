## ⚡ What is AITraining?

**AITraining** is a free, open-source collection of **6 AI books** that take you from a complete beginner to someone who builds voice robots, detects objects with cameras, and connects to powerful AI APIs — all on a Raspberry Pi.

No teacher needed. No money needed. Just a computer and curiosity.

---

## 📚 The Six Books

<div align="center">

| | Book | Level | What You'll Build |
|:---:|:---|:---:|:---|
| 🐍 | **Python for AI** | `Beginner` | Calculators, games, mini tools |
| 🧠 | **AI Fundamentals** | `Beginner` | Your first ML model |
| 🥧 | **Raspberry Pi AI** | `Intermediate` | Voice-activated AI robot |
| 👁️ | **Computer Vision** | `Intermediate` | Real-time object detector |
| 💬 | **LLMs & APIs** | `Advanced` | Chatbot with Gemini / Claude |
| 🚀 | **Advanced AI Projects** | `Advanced` | Full AI systems from scratch |

</div>

## 🔥 Projects You'll Build

```
🤖  Voice Robot         → Talk to your Raspberry Pi, it talks back using AI
👁️  Object Detector    → Camera sees and names everything in real time  
🚗  Traffic Detector   → Detects vehicles + license plates automatically
💬  AI Chatbot         → A chatbot powered by real LLM APIs you built yourself
🎙️  Speech Assistant   → Says answers out loud using text-to-speech
```

## 💡 Why This Exists

Most AI tutorials are either:
- ❌ Too expensive
- ❌ Too theoretical (boring!)
- ❌ Not made for Raspberry Pi or real hardware
- ❌ Not written for students

This repo fixes all of that. Everything here runs on a **₹4000 Raspberry Pi** and is explained the way a student would explain it to another student.
